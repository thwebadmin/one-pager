Offshore Software Development

We offer various services to take care of your IT needs while allowing you to focus on your business. The IT services practice offers the following services to our clients:

We offer our customers, shared or a dedicated Offshore Development Center (ODC) to carry out development activities that will help you meet your business objectives. We follow a well- defined and time tested System Development Lifecycle Processes which help us to deliver a high quality application within defined timelines and budget.

We offer our customers, shared or a dedicated Offshore Maintenance Center to provide software maintenance and support for their IT applications. Our processes are designed to ensure a smooth flow for an application change request, bug fixing, performance fine-tuning and other such requirements.

We offer our customers, services and support for product development, from concept to development to performance audits via engagement models that are flexible and customized to their needs.

We offer services of our Software Validation Engineers for Customers who want to enhance the effectiveness and efficiency of their Software Application or Product by focusing on accelerated and optimized testing. Our Validation Engineers have strong expertise in Functional Testing, Regression and Performance Testing.

We offer services of our Software Professionals for Customers looking out for time bound IT experts to take care of their IT needs. Our team of Software Professionals has strong expertise in various platforms for software design and development and knowledge of various tools that can accelerate your development and validation phases.
