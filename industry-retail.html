Retail

The retail industry today can be labelled as one the most complex industries. In its core the business is simplistic in nature, the complications arrive due to the way business is done and integration with other pieces/industries like manufacturing and supply chain logistics. Technology plays a big role in what a retailer can achieve, be it bigger market share, store layout, product placement or operational efficiency.

The competition is stiff and margins are razor thin. The Indian retail market is unique conundrum of aspects like FDI, highly efficient kirana stores and small producers. The landscape is ever changing.We, at Thinking Hut, possess a deep understanding of the retail environment in emerging markets, especially India.

We can help you develop, migrate and integrate your IT assets tuned to the emerging market.
