Social

At Thinking Hut, we believe that social media is an integral part of any industry today. No industry can operate in the isolation of the yesteryears with the advent of strong social media led by Facebook, LinkedIn and Twitter.

It's important for the businesses today to leverage the social media from a branding and feedback perspective. The social media is an effective way to reach out to maximum number of people at minimum cost. The social media works in a viral way at times – which means the good news as well as bad news reaches the intended and not-so-intended set of people at lightning speed. It's confusing at times because of the large number of messages and the rate at which they get generated.

The social angle is important from branding perspective – the brand quickly tanks if the buzz about you is not positive. It's also a very strong platform to obtain suggestions and feedback about the products and services and take corrective actions proactively.

Thinking Hut can help you in various ways

  • Help you understand the implication of social media for your business.
  • Help weed out messages from the noise while ensuring that the summary is comprehensive and representative of the voices being made.
  • Help you integrate your existing applications to social media and set processes to leverage the integration.
  • Help you create applications tightly integrated with social media applications like Facebook and Twitter.

"Most of the projects that are worked upon today are either social media based or have a strong social media accent."
