AchhaBachha

"AchhaBachha - The Healthy Kid" is an initiative by Thinking Hut to support a healthy child, build healthy society and a healthy world. It's an Interactive Child Care portal that will provide tools, resources and comprehensive information for parents to monitor and track their precious one's health and overall growth. Parents looking out for right child care resources and searching for information will have to look no-where else.

AchhaBachha would help parents track and monitor the growth of their children in a simple, visual and interactive way. The portal would also provide a platform for the parents to interact with like-minded people, search facility for locating healthcare services and other useful tools. It's meant for the "aware" parents with children in the age group of zero to five years.
