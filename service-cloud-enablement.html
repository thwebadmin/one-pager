Cloud Enablement

Amazon Web Services | Google App Engine | Microsoft Windows Azure

Cloud computing development offers a cost-efficient, secure alternative to traditional hardware infrastructures. Rather than managing your on-site infrastructure, cloud computing development allows you to instead concentrate on your core business. Additionally, cloud computing development eliminates business risk up front due to its scalability and elasticity.

At Thinking Hut, we understand that ultimately, the cloud is about users, not about the development itself, but that doesn't mean development can be neglected. We help you evaluate and finalize the right cloud platform for your needs. Be it Amazon Web Services, Google App Engine or MS Azure, we have strong expertise in developing and migrating applications to these platforms. With a deep understanding of the security, multi-tenancy, and manageability issues that differentiate SaaS software from on-premise software, we also help you to identify the right set of applications that can be migrated from your enterprise portfolio to start reaping the cloud benefits.
