e-Business Solutions

e-Business is a powerful tool for business transformation that allows companies to enhance their supply-chain operation, reach new markets, and improve services for customers as well as for suppliers and employees. At Thinking Hut, we offer comprehensive set of e-business solutions to facilitate transition of businesses from traditional business models to e-business models and help them in becoming modern enterprises.

We can also help you to take advantage of open source platforms that can help you to manage and expand your systems easily and cost-effectively. We provide a right solution for your business needs by leveraging the best available Open Source packages such as Joomla, nopCommerce, Drupal, Word Press and many more.

We are skilled in building complete, turn-key e-business solutions that can incorporate a number of complex functionality points, including integration with back-end catalog and legacy systems, interfacing real-time with a payment gateway, and containing a full administration site that will allow the you to easily maintain the products and content on your site.
