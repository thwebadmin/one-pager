  • Factor Freight Solutions

    FFS (Factor Freight Solutions)

    Factor Freight Solutions (FFS) is a specialist in Freight Transaction Checking. Their methodology effectively checks every line item on every invoice, cleanses it, validates it and stores it so that every paid invoice is accurate.
    Thinking Hut has developed a rule based configurable Invoice Validation system for FFS. This system empowers the user to define his own custom business rules to validate the invoiced Rates & Surcharges by the Carrier against the Client-Carrier Contract. Discrepancies, if any, are captured and claims can be raised. Subsequent to this, Thinking Hut is also developing a system for Service Check. This extension will validate whether the Carrier has met the Service levels expected for a given consignment. This would help the Client raise further claims if the service levels are not met.
